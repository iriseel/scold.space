The PREFACE, teaching the Use and Understanding of this Book of Fortune.
  1. Drag a character to the center of the volvelle
    (hint: try Kate first!)
  2. Once positioned, click the character to spin the volvelle
  3. Receive thy Fortune, or the Answer to thy Question

These character illustrations are based on the medieval-renaissance tradition of fortune-telling volvelles. Encircled by quasi-magical phrases or diagrammatic images, such devices, along with dice and cards, determined navigational routes on the high seas and astrological predictions for mundane affairs alike.
